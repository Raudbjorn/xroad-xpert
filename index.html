About Me

I'm Sveinbjörn Geirsson, a software engineer with over a decade of experience in the payment card industry, now specializing in X-Road implementations and integrations.

At Reiknistofa Lífeyrissjóða (2023-2025), I managed the setup and maintenance of X-Road security servers, creating a secure communication layer for government services. This work was crucial for regulatory reporting to the Central Bank of Iceland, including the obligations registry for mortgage lending.

My expertise encompasses X-Road security server deployment, operational monitoring implementation, performance optimization, and service integration. I'm passionate about building reliable, secure systems where attention to detail and robust implementation are paramount.

I'm proficient in Java, Python, Scala, JavaScript/TypeScript, and numerous database systems. I also have extensive experience with cloud infrastructure (GCP/AWS), containerization, and observability tools.
